#def 

***Def***.: $q \subset \mathbb{R}^{d}$ tégla,$f:Q \to \mathbb{R}$ korlátos függvény
$\mathbb{P} = \left( P^{1}, \dots, P^{d} \right)$ felosztás
$$
m(f, q) := inf \{ f(x): x \in q \}
$$
$$
M(f, q) := sup\{ f(x) : x \in q \}
$$
$$
s(f, \mathbb{P}) = \sum_{q \in \mathbb{P}} m(f, q) \cdot t_{d}(q)
$$
$$
S(f, \mathbb{P}) = \sum_{q \in \mathbb{P}}M(f, q) \cdot t_{d}(q)
$$
